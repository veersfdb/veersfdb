15 Deep, Masterful Life Quotes From Musashi Miyamoto 
1.) There is nothing  outside of yourself That can ever enable you To get better, stronger, richer,quicker, or smarter. Everything exists. Seek nothing outside of yourself.
2.) You must understand there is more than one path to the top of the mountain.
3.) Think lightly of yourself and deeply of the world.
4.) Perceive that which cannot be seen with the eye.
5.) Do not try to look for an easy life, because in this world there is no such thing.
6.) Anyone can give up, it is the easiest thing in the world to do. But to hold it together when everyone else would understand if you fell apart, that's true strength.
7.) Truth is not what you want it to be; it is what it is, and you must bend to its power or live a lie.
8.) All men are the same except for their belief in their own selves, regardless of what others may think of them.
9.) Resentment and complaint are appropriate neither for oneself nor others.
10.) If you wish to control others you must first control yourself.
11.) Do not hold onto possessions you no longer need.
12.) Do nothing that is of no use.
13.) From one thing, know ten thousand things.
14.) The important thing in strategy is to suppress the enemy's useful actions  but allow his useless actions.
15.) Today is victory over yourself of yesterday; tomorrow is your victory over lesser men.
